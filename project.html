CLASH OF GAMERS

Clash of Clans is a freemium mobile strategy game developed by Supercell. It is set in a fantasy world where players build and upgrade their villages, train troops, and battle against other players. The objective is to build and maintain a village, defend it against other players, and attack other players’ villages to earn resources and trophies.

Archer Queen

She is an immortal unit so she only has to be trained once. However, when her health is lowered in battle she must regenerate before she can be used again.

Royal Champion

The Royal Champion is the fifth Hero unlocked once the Hero Hall reaches Level 7, which is available at Town Hall level 13. She has moderately high damage.
